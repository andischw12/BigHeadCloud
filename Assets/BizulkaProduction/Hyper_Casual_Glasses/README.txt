Glasses are painted with material.All colors freely can be changed in material properties.

The temples of glasses and masks are separate objects.These objects can be disabled or adjusted.

The simplest way to add glasses to your character:
Place an empty object into the head bone. Place one of the glasses into this empty object, make sure that the positions and rotations of the glasses are reseted to "0". Move this object until the glasses fit the character head. Now you able to place any of glasses into this object and reset rotation and position of the glasses in this object.
